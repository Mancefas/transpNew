About the Company

Transportation enterprise JSC „Transpeda“ was established in 1997. Reliable and experienced drivers-expeditors work in „Transpeda“. The company can deliver various loads to Sweden, Norway, and back to Lithuania.

The trucks are tilted (88–120 m3). The company can arrange customs routine on clients' request.
